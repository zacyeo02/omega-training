Accessibility statement

We want this website to be easy to use for everyone, including people who rely on assistive technology to browse the internet.

Our approach

We have built this site with clear navigation, readable text, sensible colour contrast and full keyboard support, and we test it regularly to keep standards high.

Ongoing improvements

We are always working to improve accessibility across the site. If you come across anything that is difficult to use, we genuinely want to know about it.

Get in touch

If you experience any accessibility issues on this website, please contact us at 0151 487 0055 or c.dyson@omegalife.uk and we will do our best to help.
