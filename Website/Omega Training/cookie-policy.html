Cookie policy

This website uses a small number of cookies to help it run smoothly and to understand how it is used.

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and how you interact with it.

How we use cookies

We use essential cookies to keep the website working correctly, and may use analytics cookies to understand which pages are most useful to visitors, so we can keep improving the site.

Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Turning off non-essential cookies will not affect your ability to use the core parts of this site.
