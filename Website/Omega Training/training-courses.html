What we deliver

Training and courses

Whatever stage your organisation is at, our courses are mapped to the standards that matter and delivered by people who understand the pressures of frontline care and education.

Statutory & child protection

The foundations every team needs

Core statutory training that keeps your organisation compliant and your people safe, covering safeguarding through to first aid.

  • Safeguarding & child protectionAligned to Keeping Children Safe in Education and current statutory guidance.
  • Care CertificateThe recognised induction standard for new care staff.
  • First aidFull first aid at work, emergency first aid and paediatric first aid.
  • Prevent dutyRecognising and responding to radicalisation risks.
  • Data protection & information governanceHandling sensitive records safely and lawfully.
Regulation 32

Children's residential care

Role specific training for residential care staff and managers, built around the realities of running a home well.

  • Trauma informed practice & attachmentUnderstanding behaviour through the lens of experience.
  • Missing from care & contextual safeguardingSpotting risk early and responding well.
  • Medication safety, recording & reportingGetting the basics right, every time.
  • Supervision & reflective practiceSupporting staff wellbeing and consistent decision making.
  • Safer recruitmentBuilding a team you can trust.

Qualification pathway: Level 3 Diploma for Residential Childcare (England), with managers progressing to Level 5.

Supported accommodation

Training aligned to the Supported Accommodation (England) Regulations 2023, built for staff supporting young people towards independence.

  • Support versus careUnderstanding where the boundary sits and why it matters.
  • Risk managementSupporting independence while keeping people safe.
  • Independence skillsPractical support that builds real life skills.
  • Safeguarding, escalation & documentationKnowing when and how to act, and how to record it well.
