Why train online

The same standard of training, without leaving the floor

Not every course needs a classroom. Our platform brings the core theory, knowledge checks and certificates online, so your team can stay compliant without taking a whole shift out to do it.

Staff work through each course at their own pace, pick up where they left off, and download their certificate the moment they pass. Managers can see who has completed what, so your evidence trail stays ready for inspection.
