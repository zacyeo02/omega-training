About Omega Training

Training built on real experience

We are the training and development arm of Omega Life, a group that has been supporting children and young people since 2015.

Formed from real, day to day care

Omega Training is the training and development arm of Omega Life, a group that has been supporting children and young people since 2015.

We formalised Omega Training in 2025 to bring the same standard of care we expect in our own homes to every organisation we train. Our courses are written and delivered by people who have done the job, not just studied it, so the learning holds up against the reality of frontline care.

What sets our training apart

Trainers who have lived it

Our lead trainer has managed multi-site residential operations and sat through Ofsted inspections himself.

Built around your regulations

Every session is mapped to current standards, so your evidence trail looks after itself.

Practical, not just theory

Role play, case studies and open discussion, so people leave with tools they will actually use.

Flexible delivery

In person at your site, at ours, or through distance learning, whatever fits your team's rota.

One group, six divisions

Omega Training sits within the wider Omega Life group, alongside Omega Care, Omega Clinical, Omega Digital, Omega Education and Omega Transport.

That means our training is grounded in direct, day to day experience of running residential homes and schools, not theory borrowed from elsewhere. Our vision is simple: to be a leading provider of accredited training in health, social care and education, helping people grow professionally while raising standards across these vital sectors.
