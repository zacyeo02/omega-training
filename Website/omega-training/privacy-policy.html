Privacy policy

Omega Training, part of the Omega Life group, is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

When you make an enquiry, book a course, or contact us, we may collect your name, organisation, email address, phone number and any details you choose to share with us.

How we use your information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, arrange and deliver training, and keep you informed about courses you have booked. We do not sell your information to third parties.

Data retention

We keep personal information only for as long as it is needed for the purposes it was collected, or as required by law.

Your rights

You have the right to ask what information we hold about you, request a correction, or ask us to delete it. To make a request, contact us at c.dyson@omegalife.uk.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this policy, please get in touch at 0151 487 0055 or c.dyson@omegalife.uk.
